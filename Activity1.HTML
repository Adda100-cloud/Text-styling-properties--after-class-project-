Origami

•Traditional Japanese art of paper folding

•Originated in Japan, Edo period (1603-1868)

•Uses a single sheet of square paper

•Sometimes cuts or glue

•Basic folds: valley fold, mountain fold

•Popular models: crane, frog, butterfly, flowers

•Modern origami: complex designs, mathematical principles

•Applications: engineering, architecture, medical fields

•Benefits: enhances concentration, fine motor skills, promotes creativity, relaxation
